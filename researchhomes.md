# Minnesota Assisted Living & Senior Care Website Research

## Purpose
Research the best assisted living facility websites in Minnesota to combine their best elements into UNIIKS LLC's website.

---

## Companies Researched

### 1. Ebenezer Senior Living (ebenezercares.org)
- **Scale:** 120+ locations, 6 states, 14,000+ residents, 100+ years
- **Design:** Clean white backgrounds, dark navy text, warm accents. Minimalist and trustworthy.
- **Hero:** Large banner of seniors together. Tagline: "Everyone Deserves Community."
- **Standout:** Animated counter stats on scroll (locations, residents, years). "Six Cornerstones of Care" branded framework. Star Tribune Top Workplace badge.
- **CTA:** "Find a Community" button prominent in header.
- **Navigation:** Senior Living, Management Services, Foundation, Blog, Careers.

### 2. Presbyterian Homes & Services (preshomes.org)
- **Scale:** #1 in assisted living units nationally, 59 communities, 7,100 employees
- **Design:** WordPress + Bootstrap, professional nonprofit feel.
- **Standout:** Individual community pages with location-specific info. Optage home services brand.
- **Navigation:** Living Options > Find a Community with individual pages per location.

### 3. Ecumen (ecumen.org)
- **Scale:** Major MN nonprofit, headquartered in Shoreview
- **Design:** Green accent (#85c446) for CTAs, dark blue-gray text, white/light green backgrounds.
- **Hero:** "WHAT MAKES YOUR DAY GREAT?" - individual-centered messaging.
- **Standout:** Interactive county map for locations. Scroll-triggered header changes. ZIP code community search.
- **Navigation:** Living Spaces, Supportive Services, Getting Started, Mission Advancement.

### 4. Benedictine Living (benedictineliving.org)
- **Scale:** 30+ communities across MN, IL, MO, ND, WI
- **Design:** Clean whites, dark text, conservative trust-building palette.
- **Hero:** "Living fully, living well." Lifestyle-focused, not care-focused.
- **Standout:** Lead capture popup (name/email/zip/phone). YouTube video integration. Resident testimonials with photos and community names.
- **CTA:** "Get The Guide" lead capture.

### 5. Cassia (cassialife.org)
- **Scale:** Nonprofit, five-state operation, headquartered in Edina
- **Design:** Golden yellow highlights, white backgrounds, blues/teals for CTAs.
- **Hero:** Staff testimonial approach - humanizes the organization.
- **Standout:** 18px body text with 1.65 line-height for accessibility. Employee longevity testimonials. Equal Housing and Handicap logos. 501(c)(3) display.

### 6. Vivie (vivie.org, formerly Walker Methodist)
- **Scale:** Faith-based nonprofit since 1876, MN/ND/WI
- **Design:** Orange/coral accents, white backgrounds. Warm and approachable.
- **Hero:** Multi-generational imagery. "Transform what it means to live well."
- **Standout:** Segmented pathways by audience ("Myself," "A Loved One," "A Career"). Award badges (Minnesota's Best - Gold, Silver, Bronze). Blog carousel.
- **Navigation:** About, Foundation, Careers, Volunteer, Blog, Events.

### 7. Monarch Healthcare Management (monarchmn.com)
- **Scale:** 50+ facilities, headquartered in Eagan
- **Design:** White, navy, soft accents. Butterfly logo.
- **Hero:** "We want to be the reason you smile!" Dual care + hospitality commitment.
- **Standout:** Interactive location map. Daily rotating core value messaging. Google/Facebook review integration. Quick-apply career form.

### 8. Shorewood Senior Campus (shorewoodseniorcampus.com)
- **Scale:** Single-campus, Rochester MN
- **Design:** White backgrounds, red accent, dark grays. Clean and trustworthy.
- **Hero:** "Senior Living With Distinction." High-quality residential photo.
- **Standout:** Sticky header with persistent phone CTA. Conditional form logic. Floor plans page. Tour booking with date picker.

### 9. Brookdale Senior Living (brookdale.com) - National
- **Scale:** 645+ communities nationwide including MN locations
- **Design:** Navy blue, green accents, neutral grays. Professional yet warm.
- **Standout:** "Stories of Belonging" video testimonials. Live social media feed integration. US News recognition 4 years running. Chat button.

### 10. Sunrise Senior Living (sunriseseniorliving.com)
- **Scale:** Multiple MN locations (Edina, Buffalo, Minnetonka, Golden Valley)
- **Design:** Deep blues, whites, warm photography. Sophisticated.
- **Hero:** "POSSIBILITY RISING" / "This isn't retire, it's re-fire." Bold and empowering.
- **Standout:** Multi-step tour scheduling forms. Care Questionnaire (needs assessment). Multiple award badges. Multi-language support.

## Common Patterns Across Best Sites

### Hero Sections
- Full-width hero with emotional, lifestyle photography (never clinical)
- Concise empowering tagline (6-10 words) focused on LIVING not just CARE
- Immediate CTA ("Find a Community," "Book a Tour," "Get Started")
- Phone number visible near hero

### Color Schemes
- White/light backgrounds with navy or dark blue-gray text
- Accent colors: greens (trust/vitality), blues (trust/professionalism), warm golds/corals (warmth)
- High contrast for readability
- 16-18px minimum body text with generous line-height (1.5-1.65)

### Navigation Structure (Universal)
1. Services / Living & Care Options
2. About (mission, values, leadership, history)
3. Locations / Communities
4. Careers
5. Contact
6. Blog / Resources
7. Foundation / Giving (nonprofits)

### Must-Have Pages
1. Homepage with hero + service overview + testimonials + stats
2. Individual service pages
3. About / Mission / Values / History / Team
4. Careers portal
5. Contact with form
6. Blog / News / Resources
7. Testimonials / Stories
8. FAQ / Getting Started
9. Referrals / Intake

### CTA Strategies (by frequency)
1. "Get Started" / "Schedule a Consultation"
2. "Contact Us" / "Get in Touch"
3. "Learn More" (contextual per section)
4. Phone number always visible, sticky header
5. "Apply Now" for careers

### Service Presentation
- Card-based layouts: image + headline + brief description (<80 words)
- Lifestyle language over medical jargon
- Each service links to dedicated detail page
- Icons or branded frameworks

### Testimonials
- Short quotes (1-3 sentences) from clients/families
- Attribution with name
- Carousel/slider format (3-5 testimonials)
- Award badges as institutional social proof

### Trust Builders
- Animated statistics on scroll (years, clients served, services)
- Award badges
- License/certification displays
- Staff stories / longevity
- Equal Housing / ADA compliance badges
- Nonprofit status display

### Standout Features to Implement
- Animated scroll-triggered statistics
- Segmented user entry points ("For Individuals," "For Families," "For Case Managers")
- Sticky header with phone + CTA
- Branded care framework with memorable name
- Video integration
- Blog/resources section
- Interactive service cards with hover effects
- Smooth scroll animations
- Professional testimonial carousel
- Award/recognition display
- Accessibility compliance
